Saving Urmia Lake

About

“Saving Urmia Lake's goal is to educate people properly on Urmia Lake, so we can all work together in unity to save Urmia Lake.”

Sahand Soleimani
  • Saving Urmia Lake is an initiative taken by Sahand Soleimani to provide proper education on environmental matters, specifically ones related to Urmia Lake, and to take Action towards saving & reviving it.
  • Saving Urmia Lake has been launched in 8 languages of the world, 5 of which are the official languages of the countries surrounding Urmia Lake, which will be impacted most if Urmia Lake is destroyed, and also 3 international languages, being English, German, & French, for global availability.
